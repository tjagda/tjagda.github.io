Discussion / Study

(1/6)

Why do people want to be in a relationship? What is it for?

  • Intimate connection
  • Marriage (honour God)

Therefore a man shall leave his father and mother and be joined to his wife, and they shall become one flesh.

Genesis 2:24

(2/6)

What should you look for in someone?

Good Qualities

  • Christian*

    • Do not be mismatched with unbelievers; for what do righteousness and lawlessness share together, or what does light have in common with darkness?

    2 Corinthians 6:14 NASB

  • Independent
  • Ready to commit
  • Good character (eg. faithful, selfless, kind, generous, etc.)

Watch how they treat...

  • Strangers (eg. waiters)
  • Friends
  • Family (esp. parents)
  • Kids (esp. if you want kids)

(3/6)

Is there such thing as 'the one'?

Not really..

  • Not found in the Bible
  • God can set up opportunities, but you have a choice
  • There's no 'one'. Just 'possible ones'
  • Someone becomes the one when you marry them

(4/6)

How does one know if a relationship is worth pursuing or staying in?

  • Prayer

    • I will instruct you and teach you in the way you should go; I will counsel you with my eye upon you.

    Psalm 32:8

  • Counsel

    • Without consultation, plans are frustrated, But with many counselors they succeed.

    Proverbs 15:22

  • Logically thought about it
  • Are mentally / emotionally ready
  • Accepted each others' differences and flaws
  • Right timing in each other's lives
  • Not toxic to other parts of your life (eg. school, work, home, and self)

(5/6)

Why do some relationships last but most don't?

  • Communication
  • Humility
  • Honesty
  • Openness
  • Forgiveness
  • Fun
  • Growing together
  • Works even when together and apart
  • Continuously choosing to love when you don't feel it

(6/6)

What should one do while single?

  • Focus on self
  • Be content in God
  • Work on non-romantic relationships
  • Serve
  • Make friends
  • Be open
