Real Hope:
God Cares About You!

What is the Bible designed to give us?

  • Romans 15:4
  • Romans 15:13

It is designed to help us learn endurance and give us encouragement so we can have hope! (Romans 15:4)

The Bible gives use hope because the "God of hope" recorded it for us.

When things seem hopeless, God gives hope that serves as a steady anchor (Romans 15:13)

What makes hope possible?

  • Romans 5:3-5
  • Romans 5:6-8
  • Romans 8:28
  • Romans 8:31

The love of God shows Christians that even troubles work for our ultimate good (Rom 5:3-5)

Even before we turn to God and turn away from doing things that go against God's laws (or sin), God already loves us.

Jesus Christ took the death penalty we deserved and paid it Himself (Rom 5:6-8)

When we love God in return, He can make everything, even the difficult times, work out for our eternal good (Romans 8:28)

When God—the Creator of everything—is for us, all enemies are powerless to stop Him from blessing us! (Romans 8:31)

Does God understand our problems?

  • Hebrews 4:14-16
  • Jesus Christ experienced the pains and sorrows of human life and the temptations to do wrong. He understands and sympathizes.
  • God has given us the priceless privilege of being able to come boldly before Him in prayer in all our many times of need!
