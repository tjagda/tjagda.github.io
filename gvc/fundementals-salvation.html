Why should we care?

Should people even want to be saved?

  • God does not desire us to suffer and be punished, but to know Him
  • God loves us so much even in our current state that He sent His son to die for us

The Lord is not slow about His promise, as some count slowness, but is patient toward you, not willing for any to perish, but for all to come to repentance.

How do we become saved?

Are there any requirements?

What it isn't from..

  • Status
  • Success
  • Following rules
  • Good works

How it really goes

  • Recognize
  • Repent
  • Believe
  • Follow
  • Grow

If you declare with your mouth, “Jesus is Lord,” and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved.

Romans 10:9

How do we know we are saved (and still saved)?

Know that works don't "keep you saved"

  • Just the very word saved implies that it is a past event
  • Only happening once and not a recurring thing you have to upkeep
  • Good works can be good evidence for salvation, but does not guarantee it

Know that circumstances don't define your salvation

  • Blessings and being "perfect" aren't a signs of being saved
  • At the same time, difficulties and flaws don't mean we aren't in Christ

Know God's promise and assurance

No power in the sky above or in the earth below—indeed, nothing in all creation will ever be able to separate us from the love of God that is revealed in Christ Jesus our Lord.

Romans 8:39

What should we do after we are saved?

  • Read the Bible
  • Go to a church
  • Pray
  • Build your character
  • Grow in faith
  • Serve
