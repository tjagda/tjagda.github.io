What are tithes and offerings? Is there a difference?

  • A tithe is a specific amount (10% of your income) that you give first
  • An offering is anything extra that you give beyond that

Do you have to tithe?

No*

  • Tithing is Biblical but not mandatory
  • You don't have to strictly give 10%
  • God loves us when we give and when we don't give
  • 75% to 90% of those who go to church don't tithe (link)

What it's for

  • Not for God's benefit‚ÄîHe doesn't need our money
  • Not a way to earn God's love‚Äîbecause we already have it
  • A way to show that we trust God with our lives and our finances
  • Supports the work of the local church and pastors
  • A thing meant for our benefit because sacrificing a portion of our income reminds us to rely on God to meet our needs (and the needs of others)
