Logical Problem of Evil

  1. God is omniscient, omnipotent and omnibenevolent
  2. It's logically impossible for God and evil/suffering to both exist
  3. Evil/Suffering exist in the world
  4. Therefore, God does not exist


However, there are hidden assumptions to this conclusion

The burden of proof is too great

The burden of proof is too great

Atheists would have to prove that it is logically impossible that free will does not exist

Atheists would have to prove that it is logically impossible that God has good reasons for permitting suffering

Therefore, it is not logically impossible that both God and evil/suffering exist

"Sure, it's logically possible that God and evil can both exist.

But with so much evil, it is unlikely that God exists"

Full scope of evidence

  • If we consider only suffering, then it may seem that the existance of God is unlikely
